What is LEPRISE Theater

LEPRISE Theater is a creative and inclusive theater club where students explore the world of performing arts. The club brings together people passionate about acting, directing, and stage production, creating a vibrant community where all skill levels are welcome. Through workshops, improv sessions, and performances, members learn essential theater skills like acting techniques, voice projection, and stage presence. LEPRISE Theater also encourages personal growth by promoting confidence, teamwork, and creativity. Every season, they produce plays that allow members to shine, both on stage and behind the scenes, fostering a sense of accomplishment and camaraderie.
